01 — Access

Getting Here

1-minute walk from "Tsugaike Kogen" bus stop. Check-in is at UNPLAN Village Hakuba 1, ground floor reception.

📮 Address: 12840-1 Oaza Chikuni-Otsu, Otari-mura, Kitaazumi-gun, Nagano 399-9422
Please check in at UNPLAN Village Hakuba 1 (1F).
🚌By Public Transport
1-minute walk from "Tsugaike Kogen" bus stop.
Direct buses (Alpico Kotsu) from Nagano Station, Matsumoto Station, and Shinjuku Bus Terminal.
See timetables on the Explore Hakuba page.
🚗By Car
UNPLAN has 2 dedicated parking lots (first-come, first-served).
Available: 15:00 on check-in day — 10:00 on check-out day
If full, please use the Tsugaike Daini parking (free, ~10 min walk).
📌 Important: Please do not park or turn around in other accommodations' premises, and absolutely no street parking.

10 — House Rules

House Rules

For everyone's safety and comfort, please observe the following rules during your stay.

Safety & Security

  1. An evacuation map is displayed in each guest room. Please confirm upon arrival.
  2. When leaving your room, do not forget your passcode or key. A lost key fee of ¥5,000 applies.
  3. Only registered guests may stay. Visitors are not allowed inside rooms.
  4. The entire building is non-smoking. Please do not smoke in rooms.
  5. Do not bring or use heating/cooking devices or irons in your room.
  6. Refrain from any actions that could cause a fire.
  7. Please do not enter staff-only areas such as back-of-house or kitchen.
  8. During café hours, the 1F café space prioritises café customers.

Valuables & Lost Items

  1. Please manage your cash and valuables yourself. We are not responsible for theft or loss.
  2. Lost items are kept for 30 days (from the day of receipt or discovery), after which they are processed under applicable law.
